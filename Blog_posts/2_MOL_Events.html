Musical Camaraderie

Iota Zeta is trying to beat the heat with our other UGA Music Organizations this fall! Aizhia Poblete, our Music Org Liaison (MOL!), helped organize two organization-wide socials with the UGA chapters of Phi Mu Alpha, Kappa Kappa Psi, and Tau Beta Sigma in August.

On August 15th, we had our Music Org Back to School Cookout, where members of each org came together to share in song, food, and friendship. On August 25, we got creative together with a Tie Dye Mixer. Members of each org were able to purchase and dye an awesome 4-org shirt!

IZ values the camaraderie we share with PMA, KKY, and TBS, and we will continue to enjoy events with them in the future. We love supporting our fellow collegiate musicians!

Cookout photos courtesy of Aizhia Poblete. Tie Dye photos courtesy of KKY president Harli Cleveland.
